Got Questions?

Frequently Asked.

Everything you need to know before walking through our doors.

Do you offer a free trial before joining?

+

Yes! We offer a completely free first session with no obligation. Just fill out the form in the Contact section or message us on WhatsApp and our coaches will schedule your trial within 24 hours.

What are your gym timings?

+

We're open Monday to Saturday from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM, and Sundays from 6:00 AM to 2:00 PM. Early birds and night owls are both welcome.

Is personal training included in all memberships?

+

Personal training is included in our Transformation (Elite) plan. The Performance plan includes coaching support and diet guidance, while the Base Camp plan provides full gym access with all equipment. You can always upgrade at any time.

I'm a complete beginner — is Kaizen the right fit for me?

+

Absolutely. Many of our 500+ members started with zero gym experience. Our coaches excel at meeting you exactly where you are, building your confidence, teaching correct form, and designing a program that grows with you. You won't feel lost or judged here.

Do you provide nutrition guidance?

+

Yes. Our certified nutrition coach Shalu provides diet guidance in the Performance plan and a fully customized nutrition plan in the Transformation plan. We believe training and nutrition together produce results — not one without the other.

Where exactly is Kaizen Fitness Studia located?

+

We're located at Shubhalay Vilas, 36, Awadhpuri, BHEL, Bhopal, MP 462022. Easily accessible from BHEL Colony, Awadhpuri, and surrounding areas. Click the map in the Contact section for directions.

Can I pause or cancel my membership?

+

Yes, we offer flexible membership options. Contact us directly via WhatsApp or phone to discuss pausing, upgrading, or cancelling your plan. We believe in transparency with no hidden lock-ins.

What equipment is available at Kaizen?

+

We have a fully equipped premium facility including power racks, Olympic barbells, full dumbbell sets (2–50 kg), cable machines, a dedicated cardio zone with treadmills and assault bikes, and a functional training area with kettlebells, battle ropes, and resistance bands.

Is the gym suitable for women?

+

Absolutely. Kaizen is a welcoming, inclusive environment for everyone. A significant portion of our members are women, and our coaches are experienced in training for female goals — whether that's fat loss, toning, strength building, or postpartum fitness.

How quickly can I expect to see results?

+

Most members notice improved energy, sleep, and mood within the first two weeks. Visible body composition changes typically begin between 4–8 weeks with consistent training and nutrition. Members on our Transformation plan have seen 8–15 kg of fat loss in 3 months.
